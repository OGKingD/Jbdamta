ABOUT US

JB DAMTA is a friendly, privately run company based in Nigeria, offering specialized on-site fuel tank cleaning services to clients in a wide range of industries. Using modern technology, our highly-experienced team provides a thorough, intensive fuel tank cleaning service. We work with you to provide solutions for increased uptimes by cleaning your most hard-to-clean tanks, promising high quality, competitive pricing, prompt delivery, with an outstanding attention to details.

JB DAMTA solutions are customized to taking your preventive maintenance schedules to the next level. Our objective is to help you protect your fuel storage tanks, your fuel, and your expensive engines by disallowing entry of harmful contaminants. Our high accuracy Flow Meters and Tank Level Monitoring systems are excellent solutions for monitoring your wet stock level remotely. Our customer service is the key to our list of satisfied clients, who have been associated with us from the beginning of our operations. These clients range from Telecommunication Operators, Commercial Properties, Service Stations, Shopping Malls, Oil & Gas and Construction Industries.

JB DAMTA: your One Stop Partner for Flow Meter, Tank Calibration, Tank Cleaning, Petroleum Equipment, Storage Tank Maintenance, Fuel Filtration, Piping Installation, Tank Gauging System & Level Monitoring, Fuel Analysis, Facility Inspection and Diesel Testing Kit.
